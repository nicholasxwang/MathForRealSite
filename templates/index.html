Our Mission üöÄ

As students, we best understand what other students don‚Äôt understand. Inspired to make math exciting, intuitive, and accessible to everyone, we are dedicated to helping our students develop not only mathematical thinking and intuition, but also understand its connections with the real world. We adopted the discussion form of math circles, where everyone gets to participate and contribute to the flow of the sessions. Our goal is for students to excel at in-class math and be excited to explore math outside of class. We are a group of middle school and high school volunteers who understand what others are struggling through. We want to revolutionize the way in-class math is taught, and thus, how everyone views math.

Topics Covered ‚ú®

Arithmetics

This is a program for elementary students on fundamental arithmetics. Topics include arithmetics involving decimals, fractions, multi digit multiplication, and more.

Algebra

This is a program for students who seek help with algebra and its applications. Topics include converting problems into equations, solving linear equations, ratios, etc.

Geometry

This is a program for students who need help or want to explore more in the subject of Geometry. Topics include typical elementary-school geometry topics such as angles, similar triangles, circles, etc.

Special Topics

This is a program for advanced students who enjoy math and want to learn more advanced concepts. Topics covered include graph theory, combinatorics, and trigonometry
